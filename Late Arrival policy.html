4. Late Arrival policy

    A Transcodezy IT Solutions Pvt. Ltd., we value punctuality as it contributes to maintaining a professional work environment and ensures that projects run smoothly. Below are the guidelines regarding working hours and late arrival consequences.

    Working Hours:

    ● The official working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM , Monday to Friday.

    ● Employees are expected to be at their desks and ready to work by 9:00 AM.

    ● Breaks during the day are to be taken within the allocated time slots.


    Late Arrival Policy

    We understand that occasional delays may occur, but it’s important to follow this policy for maintaining a disciplined and efficient workspace

    ● Late Arrival Definition:Any arrivalspan after 9:00 AMwill be considered late


    Compensation for Late Arrival

    ● Time Adjustment:Employees arriving after 9:00 AM are required to compensate for the late time by extending their workday. For example, if you arrive at 9:15 AM, you will need to stay 15 minutes beyond 6:00 PM to make up for the lost time.

    ● No Overtime Eligibility:This compensatory time is not counted towards overtime. Only time worked beyond the regular working hours, after fulfilling the standard 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM schedule, is eligible for overtime


    Consequences of Non-Compliance

    Failure to adhere to the above late arrival policy will result in the following actions:

    ● First Instance:: A verbal reminder will be issued.

    ● Repeated Offenses:Written warnings may be issued if the behavior persists.

    ● Further Action:Continuous disregard for the policy may lead to disciplinary action, including possible salary deductions or suspension, depending on the severity and frequency of the violation.
